Race Timing Solution



Itemhound developed the Strider system for running events, catering to high volume races such as marathons and small fun runs alike. The system can capture up to 200 detections per second, easily detecting everyone that passes through the timing line.

Strider performs well in road races, in trail and mountain courses, and even in obstacle course challenges.

Itemhound developed the Sprint system for cycling tours and is capable of capturing high speed and high volume races. The system is designed for circuit and point to point races, whether on or off the road. Sprint Cycling System has been used for numerous cycling events here in the Philippines, including one of the biggest cycling event in the country with more than 3,000 riders.

Sprint is integrated with the Strider system for duathlons and triathlons.

Racer is a motorsport timing system designed for closed-circuit motor racing. Deviating from the rather expensive transponder system which has long been the bane of organizers, Racer uses disposable timing tags that are economical and easily replaced when a rider accidentally damages or loses the tag.

Racer has been the official timing system of the Yamaha GP One Make Race series for three consecutive years and was the official timing partner of the Yamaha ASEAN Cup 2012.
